2. Business Model

Armut operates a lead-generation / quote-comparison marketplace very similar to OferteMutare.ro:

AspectHow it works
Customer side100% free — users post service needs at no cost, receive up to 5 competitive quotes
Professional sideFree registration, then pay-per-lead (credit system) — pros buy credits to respond to requests
Revenue sourceProfessionals purchasing credits (köntör) to send quotes to customer leads
MatchingSmart matching algorithm connects requests with up to 5 relevant professionals based on location, service type, ratings, availability
Competition mechanicProfessionals compete on price, reviews, and service quality to win jobs
Booking modelSome services (e.g., cleaning) also offer direct reservation/booking alongside quotes
SubscriptionsAvailable for recurring services — e.g., weekly cleaning with 3-month or unlimited plans
OferteMutare.ro parallel: Our Pay-on-View credit system is essentially the same model — Armut validates it works at massive scale (7.5M requests/year). Key difference: we charge when the customer views the offer, Armut charges when the professional sends the offer.

How OferteMutare.ro Wins as a Challenger

  1. Niche depth beats generalist breadth — HomeRun has 60+ categories; we have ONE. Our moving experience will be 10x deeper: instant calculator, damage guarantee, inventory checklists, packing tips, floor-by-floor estimates
  2. Accumulate moving-specific reviews FAST — They have 4,689. Target 2,000+ verified moving reviews in 12 months. Quality > quantity (verified photos, detailed ratings)
  3. Dominate moving SEO — Own "firme mutări [city]" for all 41 counties + Bucharest. HomeRun competes across 60+ keyword families; we only need to win ONE
  4. Be the Romanian brand — HomeRun is a foreign platform with Turkish/Polish code artifacts. Position OferteMutare as the 100% Romanian moving platform
  5. Better pro economics — HomeRun charges "taxa de contact" + commission. Offer more attractive pricing to win pro supply side
  6. Guarantee program — 500–1,000 RON damage coverage. HomeRun has NO moving-specific guarantees
  7. Mobile apps before them — First moving-dedicated iOS + Android app in Romania
  8. Content moat — City-specific moving guides, cost calculators, company profiles — things a generalist won't build

Strategic conclusion (REVISED): OferteMutare.ro is a challenger, not a first-mover. HomeRun.ro (Armut/ProntoPro) is already here with significant infrastructure (626 moving cos., 4,689 reviews).

However, their generalist approach is our opportunity. They spread across 60+ categories with low brand awareness in Romania. We go 100% deep on moving — better UX, better pro economics, damage guarantees, city-specific content, and a genuinely Romanian brand.

The play: become the vertical champion that the generalist can't out-execute. Every review, company partnership, SEO position, and guaranteed move = a brick in our moat. Whether HomeRun stays generalist, doubles down, or acquires us — we win if we own the moving vertical.

19. Risk Factors & Regulatory Precedents

Regulatory risk (Turkey precedent, Dec 2025)

What happened: On Dec 8, 2025, armut.com was blocked by Turkey's BTK (Telecom Authority) after a court order triggered by a complaint about an ambulance service listing. The block was ISP-level (DNS/IP blocking). Lifted ~24 hours later after emergency legal action.

Impact: ~2M professionals and millions of customers temporarily lost access. Co-CEO Başak publicly responded from Milan, highlighting the economic impact.

Lesson for OferteMutare.ro:
  1. Even dominant platforms face sudden regulatory action
  2. Maintain legal counsel familiar with Romanian digital marketplace regulations
  3. Ensure service category listings comply with Romanian law (licensing requirements for regulated services)
  4. Have a crisis communication plan ready
  5. Consider hosting on Romanian/EU servers for data sovereignty
